Why Join OnlyFoods?

OnlyFoods is a student-run food cooperative at Ohio State University dedicated to combating food insecurity while providing healthy, fresh meals to our community. As a member of our team, you'll be part of a meaningful movement that makes a real difference in people's lives.

You'll gain valuable service experience, learn about food preparation and distribution, work alongside passionate peers, and contribute to a cause that matters. Whether you're interested in cooking, logistics, community outreach, or event planning, there's a place for you at OnlyFoods.
